Galaxy BRIDGE

Welcome to BRIDGE (Biomolecular Reaction and Interaction Dynamics Global Environment).
BRIDGE is a Computational Chemistry flavour of Galaxy - a webserver for preparing, simulating and analysing and visualising molecular simulations, especially molecular dynamics and free energies - developed at the Scientific Computing Research Unit.
We hope you enjoy testing the computational chemistry tools, if you find this resource useful in your research, please cite us.

Getting Started

  • Register and Login
  • Test out some tools (search for Free Energy!)
  • Try out the Free Energy workshop tutorial.

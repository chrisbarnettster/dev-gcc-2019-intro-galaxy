Welcome to BRIDGE

BRIDGE (Biomolecular Reaction and Interaction Dynamics Global Environment) is a Computational Chemistry flavour of Galaxy - a webserver for preparing, simulating and analysing and visualising molecular simulations, especially molecular dynamics and free energies - developed at the Scientific Computing Research Unit.

This BRIDGE instance is hosted at the CHPC.

We hope you enjoy testing the computational chemistry tools, if you find this resource useful in your research, please cite us.
